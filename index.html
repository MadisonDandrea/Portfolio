About Me!

I am a 15 year old genderfluid media designer. I do works of code and art. I have created various works of coding as well as some art pieces. They are mainly sketches but that is art nontheless. I aim to be an animator of some sort either for a big company or just a small little creator like myself.

More About Me <3

I have a family of my parents, aunt, and five siblings. I live with my parents, one of my brothers and my aunt. I am a pround fur parent of two ferrets, Moose and Mushu, one hamster, Em, and one cat that has adopted my dad as his parent, Max. My mom and aunt also own one cat each.
